## What is Kinfolk?

Kinfolk is an open-source, privacy-first smart display for families. Think Amazon Echo Show, but:
- ✅ Fully open-source
- ✅ Privacy-first (local processing)
- ✅ No subscriptions, ever
- ✅ Customizable & extensible

## Hardware You'll Need (Eventually)

**To run Kinfolk, you'll need:**

1. **Display:** 1080x1920 vertical touchscreen monitor (~$150-300)
2. **Computer:** Raspberry Pi 5 (4GB+) or mini PC (~$60-200)
3. **Microphone:** USB mic (~$10-50)
4. **Speaker:** USB or 3.5mm speaker (~$20-50)
5. **Camera:** (Optional) USB webcam for face recognition (~$30-70)

**Total estimated cost:** $270-670 (one-time, no subscriptions)
